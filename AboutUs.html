About Wood Bee Designs

Wood Bee Designs is a company in the South East of Ireland that hand crafts and designs beautiful, bespoke fitted kitchens. Colin Browne is the talent behind the company, and with over 30 years experience crafting beautiful designs, your kitchen is in safe hands! The kitchens are of the highest quality as Colin uses traditional woodcarving techniques and the finest locally sourced raw materials.
