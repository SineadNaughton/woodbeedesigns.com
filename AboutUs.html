About Wood Bee Designs

Wood Bee Designs is a company in the South East of Ireland that hand crafts and designs beautiful, bespoke fitted kitchens. Colin Browne is the talent behind the company, and with over 30 years experience crafting beautiful designs, your kitchen is in safe hands! The kitchens are of the highest quality as Colin uses traditional woodcarving techniques and the finest locally sourced raw materials. Why not get in touch with Wood Bee Designs today to have a chat about your new kitchen.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to provide measurements or will the designer come and measure it out?

It would be the best if you could provide measurements of your kitchen in order for our designer to come up with a preliminary design. It also shortens the process if the measurements are already provided.

How much will it cost?

The cost will very much depend on the design, size, and material. We also have to take into account the amount of time it takes to fit the kitchen. If you would like to find out more about the costs, please refer to our contact page and leave a question, we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Would the price include installation or delivery?

The price does include installation or delivery. The focus of Wood Bee Designs is to make it as easy as possible for you to enjoy your new bespoke kitchen.

What is the warranty

Quality and design would be our main focus, therefore we could proudly vouch for our quality. If there is any issue, you could contact Rosie and we will get back to you as soon as possible regarding repair and refund.

Who should I contact for after sale care?

Rosie deals with all the customer care side of things. You could reach out through email, facebook, or simply leave us your details on our contact page and we will get back to you as soon as possible .
